About us

Wonder Events are a creative events company based in Manchester. We create, design and deliver events of all shapes and sizes throughout the UK. We have over 10 years experience putting on amazing events and during that time, we’ve gotten pretty good at it!

Whether you’re a bride with dreams of that special day, a company with a large budget, or a parent wanting a truly bespoke birthday celebration for your child, we have the passion, talent and experience to make your event a unique and memorable occasion.

Why choose Wonder Events?

At Wonder Events, we take your vision and bring it to life, taking care of all the little details that take up your valuable time to ensure your event is an unforgettable experience. We achieve this through building strong relationships with all our clients and suppliers, ensuring comfort and reassurance at every level. Every event is as special to us as it is to you.
